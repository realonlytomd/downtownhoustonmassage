Massage for Men

Focused bodywork for men who need to relieve tension, recover from workouts, and get back to your responsibilities feeling steady and clear.



Professional Bodywork for Active Guys

I’m a Texas-licensed massage therapist with over 30 years experience working with men of all backgrounds — from tradesmen and professionals to weekend warriors and serious athletes.
My approach blends Swedish, sports, and deep-tissue massage techniques tailored to each man’s needs and goals.
I'll help you relax, unwind, and reset.


Sessions take place in a calm, private loft in Downtown Houston. It's conveniently located in the historic Market Square District.
